SEQUENCE DIAGRAMS INFO - ETHAN NELSON

DEFINITION:UML sequence diagrams are used to show how objects interact 
in a given situation. An important characteristic of a sequence diagram 
is that time passes from top to bottom : the interaction starts near the 
top of the diagram and ends at the bottom
